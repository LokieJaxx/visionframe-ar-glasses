VisionFrame: AI-Powered Smart Glasses

What Is VisionFrame?

VisionFrame is a next-gen pair of AI-powered smart glasses designed for people with ALS and accessibility needs. Featuring ChatGPT scripting, colorblind correction, gesture input (planned), and private bone conduction audio — it's built for all-day, everyday use.

Why It Matters

People with ALS lose the ability to speak or interact easily. VisionFrame helps restore independence with hands-free AI tools and AR overlays — in a discreet, wearable form.
